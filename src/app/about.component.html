BiaSearch Mission and Goal

It is sometimes hard to know whether or not the news you are receiving is impartial. In fact, every source owns an innate bias. In recent times, it seems many people and new sources have split to the extremes of the political spectrum. Some are certainly more extreme than others, but regardless, one should not limit themselves to one source. And so, we created BiaSearch with the goal of expanding the news a person receives.

Instead of labeling a single source as more or less accurate than any other, BiaSearch enables a person to read a variety of sources in the hopes of establishing a more impartial intake of news. We hope that people will acknowledge the biases and fallacies in our daily news, and use BiaSearch to become more informed citizens.
